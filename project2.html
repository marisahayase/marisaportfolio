UX Persona Cards

These UX persona cards were created for PSU's Office of Academic Innovation (OAI) design team to use with faculty while designing courses. These persona cards and several others were created with real student data collected from PSU’s student body to provide inclusive student scenarios and perspectives, ultimately keeping students centered in faculty/OAI’s work. Each persona card features a vector-illustrated icon and an easy-to follow array of traits for users, and includes a data story-card to give context to the data found accompanying each persona.
