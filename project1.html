Kalo Branding

Kalo (taro in Hawaiian) is inspired by my home state of Hawai'i and based around the concept of a chocolate-free taro bar. Following the idea of a chocolate-substitution, the line of products also include taro latte mix and taro marshmallows (a replacement for hot chocolate and plain marshmallows!). With flowing lines reminiscent of waves and hand-drawn type, Kalo is sure to catch the attention of anyone looking to try something new, or for those missing the taste of home like I am.
